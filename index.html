My Top 5 Animes!

Apothecary Diaries

An apothecary by the name of Maomao gets kidnapped and is forced to work in the rear palace of the emperor. There she works as a palace servant. Follow Maomao's journey as she takes care of the consorts and unravels mystery after mystery.

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners

Nighty City a place where drugs, body modifications, and crimes go rampant. David a kid living in the city having nothing decides to become an Edgerunner, a mercenary outlaw. Through body modifications and technology will David have what it takes to become a legend of Night City or die trying.

Horimiya

The love story of Hori and Miyamura takes place! Watch this entertaining romcom full of interesting side characters!

Sword Art Online

VR Technology has finally become a thing. Kazuto Kirigaya sets foot in the first VRMMORPH Sword Art Online. But the game isn't what it seems to be?

Kimetsu no Yaiba

The Demon Slayer Corp founded to protect the civilians of the nation from the grips of demons. Tanjiro lives far from the city in the forest but one day comes home to his family slain by demons except for one of his siblings, who turned into a demon! Follow Tanjiro as he steels his resolve and joins the Demon Slayer Corp while trying to find a cure for his sister.
